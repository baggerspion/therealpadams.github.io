The Digger Spy?

"Baggerspion: The urge to peek into boarded-up construction sites."

As a software engineer, I love working with technology. Moreover, I am completely fascinated with how technology is created: team dynamics, process, culture...

"Baggerspion" is a completely made-up German word which sums-up my approach to engineering leadership. I enjoy peeking into engineering teams, understanding how they work. Only once I have studied can I help to remove obstacles and improve practices and performance.
